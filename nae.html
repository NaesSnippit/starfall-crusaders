A plucky scientist with a knack for trying anything once, Naethan (Nae) Odom is fierce when it comes to using his imagination.

Calling his skills unparalleled, he’d claim, would be an understatement for those who also indulge in his many hobbies.

One of said hobbies is none other than hypnosis, which at one point he used to attempt to cure his insomnia.

Although it didn’t work, he’s eager to learn what other magic there is out there to offer.
