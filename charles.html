CHARLES COUCKEE: STARFALL CRUSADER

← RETURN

Charles Couckee, 19. He/Him. Ted’s childhood best friend.

Charles has a huge heart and the absolute worst anxiety of anyone you’ll meet. He’s afraid of being abandoned, and always tries to go the extra mile to make people happy—sometimes too far.

He’s a chronic worrier who rarely sees himself as good enough, but when things get tough, he’s surprisingly dependable.

Though not flashy like some of the others, he holds the Starfall Crusaders together — both emotionally and tactically. He also hits things with a comically large hammer. It’s very therapeutic.
