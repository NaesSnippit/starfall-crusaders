CHARLES COUCKEE

← RETURN

Charles Couckee, 19. He/Him. Ted’s childhood best friend.

Charles has a huge heart and the absolute worst anxiety of anyone you’ll meet. He’s afraid of being abandoned, and always tries to go the extra mile to make people happy—sometimes too far. Charles is a worrier who rarely sees himself as good enough, but he’s surprisingly reliable when the chips are down.

Though he isn't as flashy as the others, he holds the team together. He also hits things with a comically large hammer.
