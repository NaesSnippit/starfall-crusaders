STELLAR SKYROCKETT: STARFALL CRUSADER

← RETURN

Stellar Skyrockett, 275+. She/Her. One of the few Wish Dragons left — and bearer of the comet’s ancient will.

Kind, nurturing, and softly radiant, Stellar is the one who chose the four blessed by the Wish Comet. Why this generation skipped a fall? She’s not telling… and there’s a sadness in her eyes when asked.

She treats the team like her children, even when they don’t treat her like family. Her warmth is endless, her patience deep. But don’t mistake gentleness for weakness — there’s an old, stellar fire behind her calm.

She doesn’t fight unless she must. But when she does, it’s with elegance, grace, and the quiet strength of a star that refuses to burn out.

Whatever secrets she holds, she guards them for love. And maybe… to protect them from a truth they’re not ready for.
