✦ DIAMOND’S BLACKJACK BOOTH

Booth #212 — COMET FESTIVAL, 2025

Looking for an easy win? Booth #212 has just what you need: a simple blackjack table run by a sweet 9-year-old named Diamond. She smiles, she shuffles, she deals — what could go wrong?

Games are just $10 per play. Beat the dealer, and you win cold, hard cash. Seriously. Diamond pays out from a glittery tin lunchbox labeled “NOT A BANK.”

She looks innocent. She even wears nerdy round glasses. But don’t let that fool you — rumor has it she’s never lost a game all weekend. Some say her deck's just lucky. Others say she's got tricks in those sleeves.

Still, a few lucky winners have walked away with more than $100 in a single session. Will you be one of them?

House Rules
  • $10 per hand — exact change only, no IOUs.
  • No touching the cards. Seriously. Don’t.
  • Blackjack pays 2:1, unless Diamond says otherwise.
  • If she catches you counting cards, you owe her bubble tea.
  • All disputes resolved by a coin toss… flipped by Diamond.
