KNOWN DETAILS

She appeared, shimmered, and vanished — all before her fourteenth birthday. Backed by her father, **Virgil Odom**, one of the most powerful names in music, Diamond was launched into stardom at just 8 years old. Her real name? Still unknown. Her last confirmed public appearance? The 2019 tour finale in Tokyo — no photos since. Only holograms. Pre-recorded interviews. Silence.

The world still hums her choruses... but no one knows where she is now.

ARCHIVAL BLUR • COMET FESTIVAL, 2015

Diamond, 9 — attitude sharper than her card corners.

While crowds danced under fireworks, Diamond ran a card table in the dark corners of the square. Streetlight glinting off fake gems, she dealt with the grace of a magician and the menace of a girl who already knew the odds were always in her favor.

Charles Couckee, all of 8 and full of confidence, dropped $300 on a game of Queen of Hearts. His friend Ted begged him not to. Diamond just smiled.

"Same rules as always," she purred, flipping three cards down. "You find her, you win. You miss... I buy dinner."

No one saw where the Queen went. But Diamond left with the cash — and a new legend stitched into the seams of the festival forever.
