PERCY FELIGUIRE: STARFALL CRUSADER

← RETURN

Percival "Percy" Feliguire, 19. He/Him. Bisexual. Gaoyama exile from the shattered realm of Aegis Mountain.

Cast out from his home dimension for reasons he won't fully explain (and you probably wouldn’t believe), Percy now drifts with the Starfall Crusaders, claiming it’s “temporary” — though he’s been here longer than most.

Armed with razor wit and a perpetual smirk, he challenges everyone — not because he’s malicious, but because arguing is the closest thing he has to joy. If he's not picking apart your logic, he's probably asleep or bored.

He's convinced Stellar’s going to stab them all in the back. He’ll say it with a laugh, but there’s steel behind the joke. Underneath the sarcasm is someone who's been betrayed one too many times… and isn't ready to trust again.

He fights like he talks: clever, unpredictable, and never quite where you expect him to be. Whether he's an ally or a liability depends entirely on the day — and your tolerance for snark.
